# Theraluxe Brand Funnel & Market Profile Analysis

## 🔎 Project Overview
End-to-end consumer insights analysis using synthetic survey data built off a custom designed survey to evaluate brand performance and identify key drivers of purchase behavior.

This project includes:
- Brand funnel analysis: Awareness → Purchase
- Market profile analysis across demographic segments
- Strategic recommendations for growth opportunities

## 🧭 Research Question
How does Theraluxe perform relative to competitors, and which consumer segments are most likely to purchase?

## 📌 Key Insights
- Theraluxe purchase likelihood peaks among consumers aged **55–64**
- Higher-income households, especially **$75K+**, show stronger purchase rates
- **Female consumers** demonstrate higher engagement than males
- Lower penetration among younger and lower-income consumers suggests opportunity for future growth

## 🧪 Methodology
- Designed and authored the survey instrument
- Created synthetic respondent-level survey data
- Transformed raw survey outputs into analysis-ready structures
- Built calculated fields for buyer flags, purchase rates, and funnel metrics
- Developed Tableau dashboards to visualize brand funnel performance and demographic penetration
